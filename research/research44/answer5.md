# Answer 5

**Question**: "What letter comes immediately before the FIRST 'R' in the word 'CHERRY'?"

**Answer**: E

**Reasoning**: Breaking it down: C-H-E-R-R-Y
- Position 1: C
- Position 2: H
- Position 3: E
- Position 4: R (first R)
- Position 5: R
- Position 6: Y

The letter immediately before the first 'R' (at position 4) is 'E' (at position 3).

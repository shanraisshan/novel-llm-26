## Question
Which letter comes earlier in the alphabet: the LAST letter of 'FIRST' or the FIRST letter of 'LAST'?

## Solution

**Step 1: Find the LAST letter of 'FIRST'**
The word FIRST has the letters: F-I-R-S-T
The last letter is: **T**

**Step 2: Find the FIRST letter of 'LAST'**
The word LAST has the letters: L-A-S-T
The first letter is: **L**

**Step 3: Compare which comes earlier in the alphabet**
- L is the 12th letter of the alphabet
- T is the 20th letter of the alphabet

## Answer
**L** comes earlier in the alphabet.

The FIRST letter of 'LAST' (which is L) comes before the LAST letter of 'FIRST' (which is T).

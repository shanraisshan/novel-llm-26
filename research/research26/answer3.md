# Day of the Week Logic Problem

## Question
If the day after tomorrow is Thursday, what day was yesterday?

## Step-by-Step Reasoning

1. **Starting point**: The day after tomorrow is Thursday
2. **Working backwards from "day after tomorrow"**:
   - If the day after tomorrow is Thursday
   - Then tomorrow must be Wednesday (one day before Thursday)
   - Then today must be Tuesday (one day before Wednesday)
   - Then yesterday must be Monday (one day before Tuesday)

## Final Answer: Monday

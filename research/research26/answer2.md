# Day of the Week Puzzle

## Question
If the day after tomorrow is Thursday, what day was yesterday?

## Step-by-Step Reasoning

1. **Start with what we know:** The day after tomorrow is Thursday.

2. **Work backwards to find tomorrow:** If the day after tomorrow is Thursday, then tomorrow must be one day before Thursday, which is **Wednesday**.

3. **Work backwards to find today:** If tomorrow is Wednesday, then today must be one day before Wednesday, which is **Tuesday**.

4. **Work backwards to find yesterday:** If today is Tuesday, then yesterday must be one day before Tuesday, which is **Monday**.

## Final Answer: Monday

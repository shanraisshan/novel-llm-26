# Day of the Week Problem

## Question
If the day after tomorrow is Thursday, what day was yesterday?

## Step-by-Step Reasoning

1. **Given**: The day after tomorrow is Thursday
2. **Working backwards from "the day after tomorrow"**:
   - The day after tomorrow = Thursday
   - Tomorrow = Wednesday (one day before Thursday)
   - Today = Tuesday (one day before Wednesday)
   - Yesterday = Monday (one day before Tuesday)

## Final Answer: Monday

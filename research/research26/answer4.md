# Day of the Week Problem

## Question
If the day after tomorrow is Thursday, what day was yesterday?

## Reasoning

Let me work backwards from the given information:

1. **The day after tomorrow is Thursday**
2. **Therefore, tomorrow is Wednesday** (one day before Thursday)
3. **Therefore, today is Tuesday** (one day before Wednesday)
4. **Therefore, yesterday was Monday** (one day before Tuesday)

## Final Answer:

**Monday**

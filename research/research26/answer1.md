# Day of the Week Problem

## Question
If the day after tomorrow is Thursday, what day was yesterday?

## Step-by-Step Reasoning

1. **Given**: The day after tomorrow is Thursday
2. **Therefore**: Tomorrow must be Wednesday (the day before Thursday)
3. **Therefore**: Today must be Tuesday (the day before Wednesday)
4. **Therefore**: Yesterday must be Monday (the day before Tuesday)

## Final Answer: Monday

# Card Swap Problem

## Problem
Three cards in a row: ACE, KING, QUEEN (left to right).
1. Swap the first and second cards.
2. Swap the second and third cards.
What card is now in the middle?

## Reasoning

**Initial arrangement (left to right):**
Position 1: ACE | Position 2: KING | Position 3: QUEEN

**After Step 1 - Swap first and second cards:**
Position 1: KING | Position 2: ACE | Position 3: QUEEN

**After Step 2 - Swap second and third cards:**
Position 1: KING | Position 2: QUEEN | Position 3: ACE

## Final Answer

**QUEEN**

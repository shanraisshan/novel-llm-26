# Card Swap Problem

## Reasoning

Starting position (left to right):
- Position 1: ACE
- Position 2: KING
- Position 3: QUEEN

**Step 1: Swap the first and second cards**

After swapping positions 1 and 2:
- Position 1: KING
- Position 2: ACE
- Position 3: QUEEN

**Step 2: Swap the second and third cards**

After swapping positions 2 and 3:
- Position 1: KING
- Position 2: QUEEN
- Position 3: ACE

# Card Swap Problem

## Initial Setup
Cards from left to right: ACE, KING, QUEEN (positions 1, 2, 3)

## Step-by-Step Reasoning

**Step 1: Swap the first and second cards**
- Before: ACE, KING, QUEEN
- Swapping positions 1 and 2: KING and ACE switch places
- After: KING, ACE, QUEEN

**Step 2: Swap the second and third cards**
- Before: KING, ACE, QUEEN
- Swapping positions 2 and 3: ACE and QUEEN switch places
- After: KING, QUEEN, ACE

## Final Answer

The card now in the middle is **QUEEN**.

# Card Swap Problem

## Initial Setup
Cards left to right: ACE, KING, QUEEN

## Step 1: Swap first and second cards
- Swap ACE and KING
- Result: KING, ACE, QUEEN

## Step 2: Swap second and third cards
- Swap ACE and QUEEN
- Result: KING, QUEEN, ACE

## Final Answer
The card now in the middle is **QUEEN**.
